About Me

Hello! I am Bisma Siddiqui,
the founder of Sprouting Together.

Gardening has been a family passion of ours and has translated into my every day life. With the help of my parents, who are both avid gardening-lovers, I started this project for the betterment of my community. I have received an immense ammount of support and I am eternally grateful for everyone who has contributed to the success of Sprouting Together. Planting Seeds, Cultivating Community. Without my community, I would have no seeds to plant.
