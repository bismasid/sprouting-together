What Are Community Gardens?

Community gardens are shared plots of land where neighbors can come together and tend to plants, flowers, and harvest fruits and vegetables. These gardens strengthen community bonds and promote healthy lifestyles. Low-income communities are at the highest risk for obesity due to the convenience of fast food and the lack of affordable food options. With the option of community gardens, residents are able to plant and grow their own produce, giving everyone an equal opportunity in achieving a well-balanced and nutritous diet.
